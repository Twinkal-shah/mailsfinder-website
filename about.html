About Mailsfinder

We're on a mission to make email finding and verification accessible to everyone with the most affordable rates in the industry.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Mailsfinder was born out of frustration with expensive email finding tools that charged premium prices for basic functionality. Our founders, experienced in sales and marketing, knew there had to be a better way.

We built Mailsfinder with one simple goal: provide the most accurate email finding and verification service at prices that don't break the bank. Today, we're trusted by thousands of SDRs, BDRs, and outbound agencies worldwide.
